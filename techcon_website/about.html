Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small regional gathering of developers and has since grown into an internationally recognized event attracting thousands of attendees from around the world. Over the years, it has become a hub for innovation, knowledge sharing, and networking.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to empower tech professionals, students, and enthusiasts through impactful discussions, hands-on workshops, and inspiring talks. We believe in inclusive growth, sustainable innovation, and shaping the future of technology responsibly.
